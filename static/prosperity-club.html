Why the club exists

Prosperity Club is the opposite: every column is free, every master-class video is free, and every question gets a direct answer.

How to make better money decisions

Start with what you have, not what you wish you had. Three habits, in order:

Cash on hand, weekly
Track every dollar that moves through your account for one month. The picture you draw from that month is the only one that matters.
Insurance before investment
Renters / health / disability / term life. The downside protection has to exist before the upside math is allowed to compound.
Read before you sign
Every policy. Every contract. Every "first year free" trial. The fee schedule is where the answer is.
