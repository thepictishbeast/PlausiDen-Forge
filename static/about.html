How we work

PlausiDen is small on purpose. The platform is opinionated because every dilution we resisted was a tradeoff we did not want to ship. We pick the harder typed seam over the convenient string-keyed escape hatch, and we let the compiler reject anything else.

We do not believe in roadmaps that promise a feature in eighteen months. We believe in shipping small, audited, reproducible changes — and in letting the audit chain speak for itself when a customer asks what changed between two builds. The chain hash is the proof; the screenshot diff is the corroboration.

Principles

What we won't compromise on.

Discipline first

Tests + docs + audit on every commit. Codified in scripts/discipline-check.sh --strict.

Typed contracts at every boundary

If the types compile, the system doesn't break at runtime.

Reproducible builds

Same input → same hash. Independently verifiable; not a trust-the-vendor claim.

Privacy by default

Self-host everything. No third-party fonts, no analytics, no telemetry, no CDNs.
