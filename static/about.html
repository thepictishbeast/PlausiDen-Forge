About SkillShots

Voted skill battles. Single-take video. Pots split by your crew.

How it works

A challenge declares a measurable skill (half-court shot, 5-stair kickflip, sub-18 cube solve).

Players upload one continuous take. No edits, no second chances.

Voters compare entries and pick a winner. The pot splits at round close.

Rules of the game

Every entry is one continuous take. Cuts disqualify.

720p minimum video. Sub-720p rejected at upload.

Spec disputes go to a re-vote. Repeat offenders get rate-limited.

Why we built this

Skill is performable. Performance should be voted, not algorithmically rewarded.

No engagement loops. No infinite scroll. Just: post a take, get judged, split a pot.
