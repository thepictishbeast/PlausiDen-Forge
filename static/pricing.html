Pricing

Pay for compute, not for seats.

Free forever for solo developers. Team plans scale by build-minute and storage — never by user count. Bring your own LLM provider; we never see your prompts.

Plans, by what changes between them.

Every plan ships the same substrate: 166 primitives, 14 themes, reproducible builds, multi-network publish, signed Merkle-chained audits, the Crucible captcha, Forge-auth, and bring-your-own LLM. What changes between plans is build-minute quota, storage, support latency, and access to the compliance-grade audit channel.

We never charge per page, per visitor, or per primitive. Page count and traffic are uncapped. If you outgrow Solo it is not because someone clicked your site — it is because your team is shipping more compute.

Pricing questions

Is there a free trial?

Solo is free forever. Team's first three seats are free. No card on file, no expiry.

What's a build-minute?

A minute of CPU time spent in forge build. A typical 100-page site builds in 15-60 seconds.

Do you charge per page or per visitor?

Neither. Page count + visitor count are uncapped. We charge by compute use only.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Walk away with your content (it's JSON in git) and the audit reports.

Beta discount

During beta, Team plan seats are free with no expiry. Lock in the rate by signing up before Forge GA.
