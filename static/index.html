What we cover.

Four pillars of financial education for working- and middle-class readers in the US and Canada.

Social class

Learning about social class allows you to better understand where you stand, so that you can take the right steps towards prosperity. From income to education levels to job opportunities, social class can significantly impact your day-to-day life.

Insurance

Insurance for your home, car, and other valuables protects what you own. Track expenses, store documents safely, and consider working with a financial advisor to plan for retirement.

Investing

Investing can seem complex and intimidating, but understanding the basics can set you on a path to smarter financial decisions. Learn about types of investments, your goals, and diversification.

About Deborah

A licensed insurance agent who sells life, health, and property/casualty products. Prosperity Club was established by a passionate woman who found her way through a bleak financial situation by educating herself in finance.

About Prosperity Club.

Prosperity Club accesses experts in different fields to help you understand the roadblocks that keep you from succeeding and what you can do about it. The great thing about online education is that we help you get the financial education you need through our own educational resources or that of others we find beneficial.

For people moving up the socio-economic ladder, you will have the opportunity to learn how to manage income, invest safely, and find the educational help you need. Prosperity Club works as a repository of financial knowledge and education where you can learn how to better manage your money.

Prosperity Club helps uncover the why of expenses and identifies what actions you can take to reduce them without changing your lifestyle. Budgets do not resolve financial spending or obligation — attaching dollar amounts to each expense is not how to reduce expenses. Tracking expenses uncovers the underlying reasons why you are spending on certain things.

How we help.

Three commitments that shape every article, every Masterclass week, and every Risk Management consultation.

Better decisions

Our prosperity educators teach strategies that reduce liabilities and increase discretionary income — so you stop saying "I will never make enough money to pay my bills."

Cash on hand

Have enough money to pay for the necessities, build an emergency fund, invest, and enjoy life. Keeping most of your income is the most important step to being prosperous and planning for retirement.

Risk before return

Disability, life, health, home/renters, auto — covered honestly, including which products are oversold and which are quietly load-bearing. The foundation under every investment decision, not an after-thought.
