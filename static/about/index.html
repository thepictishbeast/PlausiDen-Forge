About

Hi, I am Deborah Armstrong.

Licensed insurance agent. Independent financial educator. Founder of Prosperity Club. I teach working- and middle-class readers what the wealthy already know about money — in plain language, with no upsells.

Contact Us

Prosperity Club was founded by a passionate woman who found her way through a bleak financial situation by educating herself in finance. After decades of learning what works, what fails, and what the financial-services industry quietly does not tell ordinary people, I built this site to make that knowledge accessible to readers who don't have a private banker on speed dial.

Who this is for.

Audience.

Working class
Readers paid hourly or with a tight salary band.If your money runs out before the month does, the first chapters of this site are for you — debt strategy, expense tracking that actually works, and which insurance products to buy first.
Middle class
Readers earning enough to invest but not enough to hire.The Investing Masterclass is built for you specifically. Most personal-finance media is either for people who can't pay their bills or for people who can hire a private advisor. This is for everyone in between.
Moving up
Readers transitioning between income tiers.The strategy changes substantially when income changes. The same advice that worked at $40k can be the wrong advice at $90k. The site addresses both starting positions and the transitions between them.
Independent
Readers who want education, not a sales pitch.Prosperity Club does not sell investments and does not push particular products. Insurance is the only product I'm licensed to sell, and I will tell you when that fits and when it doesn't.

What I learned the hard way.

The financial-services industry is structured to extract fees from the people least equipped to spot them. Annuity surrender charges. Variable-rate credit cards. Whole-life policies sold to people who needed term life. The wealthy avoid these traps because they have the time and the network to evaluate them. Everyone else gets sold them.

Prosperity Club teaches you to evaluate them yourself. The Investing Masterclass walks through the structure of every common financial product, who profits when you buy it, when it actually serves you, and how to spot the version that doesn't. Plain language, real examples, no jargon-for-jargon's-sake.
