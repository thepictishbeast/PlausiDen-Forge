Privacy Policy — Prosperity Club

Privacy Policy.

Prosperity Club takes confidentiality seriously. This page explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and how long we keep it. It is written in plain language because the financial-services industry has spent too long writing privacy policies that nobody can read.

What we collect.

Categories of personal information.

Contact
Name + email + phone (when you provide them).Only collected when you explicitly send it — through the contact form, a membership signup, or a one-to-one consultation request. We never harvest contact information from third parties.
Account
Membership tier + billing identifiers.If you become a paying member ($5/month Investing Masterclass) we store the membership tier locally and the payment-processor token. We do not store credit-card numbers — those live with Stripe.
Usage
Server logs + page views.Aggregate counts of which pages were visited, in what order, and from what country. No cross-site tracking pixels, no advertising network identifiers, no third-party analytics. Retention: 30 days, then deleted.
Communications
Your messages to us, our replies to you.Kept for as long as the conversation is active and for a reasonable period afterward for reference. Encrypted at rest. Never shared with anyone except the educator handling your question.

What we do not collect.

We do not collect your social security number, your account balances, your investment positions, your tax returns, or any other detailed financial information. The Investing Masterclass is educational; it does not require you to share your finances with us. If a particular consultation requires that level of detail, we will tell you in advance and you may decline.

How we use what we collect.

Purposes.

Deliver
Run the website, the Masterclass, and the consultations.Operational use: send you the lessons you signed up for, answer the questions you sent, process the membership payments you authorised.
Improve
Understand which lessons land and which don't.Aggregate page-view counts only. We never look at the behavior of an individual member.
Comply
Meet legal and regulatory obligations.Tax records, anti-fraud requirements, and any disclosure that's compelled by valid legal process. We will tell you when we are compelled unless prohibited from doing so.

Who we share with.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not rent it. We do not trade it. The third parties that touch your data are the payment processor (Stripe), the email-delivery service (for the Masterclass lessons), and the website host. Each is bound by a data-processing agreement that mirrors the protections in this policy.

Your rights.

What you can ask us to do.

Access
See everything we have about you.Email us and we will send you the records we hold about you, in a portable format, within 30 days.
Correct
Fix anything that's wrong.If a record we hold is inaccurate, tell us and we will correct it. If we can't correct it, we will explain why.
Delete
Have your data removed.You can ask us to delete your data. We will, except where we are legally required to retain it (tax records, payment receipts). We will tell you what we kept and why.
Object
Stop a use you do not want.If a particular use of your data is uncomfortable, tell us. Where the use is legally optional, we will stop. Where it is legally required, we will explain.

How long we keep things.

Server logs: 30 days. Contact records: 3 years from last interaction. Membership and payment records: as long as legally required (typically 7 years for tax purposes), then deleted. Anything else: only as long as needed for the purpose it was collected, then deleted.

Contact.

Privacy questions: deborah@prosperityclub.com. We will respond within five business days. If you want a written acknowledgement first, ask in your email and we will send one.
