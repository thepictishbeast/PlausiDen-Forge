Why these methods

Forge-auth ships nine typed AuthMethod variants. Operators pick the mix per tenant.

Passkey + WebAuthn

Phishing-resistant by construction. The signature is bound to your device + the origin.

Magic link + SMS OTP

Server never stores a password. Lower phishing resistance, higher recoverability.

Password + MFA

Classic salted Argon2id with mandatory MFA (TOTP / WebAuthn / SMS / Email OTP / backup codes).

Bring your own provider

forge-auth wraps a typed AuthProvider trait. Postgres-Argon2id, vault-WebAuthn, OAuth-2 / OIDC against any IdP — pick your impl, plug it in.
